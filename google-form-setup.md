# Google Form Setup — Ball Pit Photo Booth Inquiry

Go to https://forms.google.com → Create new form with these fields:

## Form Title
**Book Your Ball Pit Experience ✨**

## Form Description
Tell us about your event and we'll put together a custom quote within 24 hours.

---

## Fields (in order)

### 1. Your Name *
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes

### 2. Email Address *
- Type: Short answer (email validation)
- Required: Yes

### 3. Phone Number
- Type: Short answer
- Required: No

### 4. Event Type *
- Type: Dropdown
- Options:
  - Wedding
  - Corporate Event / Team Building
  - Brand Activation / Product Launch
  - Trade Show / Conference
  - Birthday Party
  - Other

### 5. Estimated Event Date *
- Type: Date
- Required: Yes

### 6. Event Location (City/Venue) *
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes

### 7. Estimated Guest Count *
- Type: Dropdown
- Options:
  - Under 50
  - 50-100
  - 100-200
  - 200-500
  - 500+

### 8. Which package interests you?
- Type: Multiple choice
- Options:
  - Essentials (2hr — $800)
  - Premium (3hr — $1,200)
  - Corporate (4hr — $1,800)
  - Not sure yet — help me decide

### 9. Are you interested in any of these add-ons?
- Type: Checkboxes
- Options:
  - Custom ball colors (match your theme/brand)
  - Branded photo overlays (logo on every photo)
  - 360° video capture
  - Instant photo prints for guests
  - Social media sharing station
  - Props & accessories

### 10. How did you hear about us?
- Type: Dropdown
- Options:
  - Google Search
  - Instagram
  - TikTok
  - Wedding Planner / Event Planner
  - Venue Recommendation
  - Friend / Word of Mouth
  - Facebook
  - Other

### 11. Anything else we should know?
- Type: Paragraph
- Required: No

---

## Form Settings
- Collect email addresses: Yes
- Send respondent a copy: Yes
- Confirmation message: "Thanks! We'll get back to you within 24 hours with a custom quote. 🎉"
- Link responses to a Google Sheet for tracking

## After Creating
1. Copy the form's embed URL
2. Replace `GOOGLE_FORM_URL_HERE` in the website's index.html
3. Set up email notifications for new responses
