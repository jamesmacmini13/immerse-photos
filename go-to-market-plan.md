# Ball Pit Photo Booth — 0→1 Go-To-Market Plan

## The Business
Premium ball pit photo booth rental for **weddings** and **corporate events**. We don't have inventory yet — step one is validating demand and booking our first customers.

## Strategy: Sell First, Build Second
Don't buy a ball pit until someone's paid a deposit. Use the site + portfolio to look legit, take bookings, then fulfill.

## Phase 2: First Leads (Weeks 2-4)
### Free / Low-Cost Channels
1. **Wedding vendor directories** (free listings):
   - The Knot (claim free listing)
   - WeddingWire (free vendor profile)
   - Zola vendor marketplace
   - Local wedding Facebook groups — post offering "launch pricing"

2. **Corporate event directories**:
   - Eventective (free listing)
   - GigSalad (free tier)
   - Thumbtack (pay per lead, but targeted)
   - Peerspace (partner/add-on)

3. **Local outreach** (free, high-conversion):
   - Email/DM 20 local wedding planners: "We're launching a ball pit photo booth — want to partner? You get commission on referrals."
   - Email/DM 10 local event venues: "We'd love to be on your preferred vendor list. Can we do a free demo setup?"
   - Email/DM 5 corporate event planners in your area

4. **Social media**:
   - Post portfolio content on Instagram (3x/week)
   - Reels/TikToks of ball pit content (even curated/competitor — tag as inspo)
   - Join local wedding planning Facebook groups
   - Post in r/weddingplanning, r/eventplanning (value-add, not spam)

### Paid (Small Budget, $100-300)
5. **Facebook/Instagram ads** targeting:
   - Engaged couples within 50mi (wedding)
   - Event planners, HR managers, marketing managers (corporate)
   - Lookalike audiences from inquiry form submissions
   
6. **Google Ads** on high-intent keywords:
   - "ball pit photo booth rental [city]"
   - "unique wedding entertainment [city]"
   - "corporate event photo booth [city]"

## Phase 3: First Booking → Proof (Weeks 4-8)
- **Launch pricing**: 30-40% off standard rates for first 3 bookings
- **Require permission to photograph** — real event photos are 10x more valuable than stock
- **Get video testimonials** from first clients (competitors don't have these)
- **Case study** after first event with photos, setup shots, guest reactions

## Phase 4: Scale (Month 3+)
- Raise prices to market rate ($1,500-2,500/event)
- SEO content: blog posts ("Best Wedding Entertainment Ideas 2026", "Corporate Team Building Activities That Actually Work")
- Referral program: $200 per booked referral to planners/venues
- Expand to adjacent markets: birthday parties, brand activations, festivals

## Key Metrics to Track
- Website visits → Form submissions (target: 5-10% conversion)
- Form submissions → Booked calls (target: 50%)
- Calls → Deposits (target: 30%)
- Goal: **3 bookings in first 60 days**
